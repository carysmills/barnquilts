Barn quilts of Prince Edward County

PEC has more than 150 barn quilts. Some are simple geometric shapes, others depict the Canadian flag and some illustrate nature.

The local Barn Quilt Trail operates under the auspices of the Ontario Barn Quilt Trails which is part of a North American network founded in Ohio in 2001.

In PEC, the area with the most barn quilts is Wellington. Stars are often depicted on the quilts.

West of Wellington is the area with the second most quilts.
